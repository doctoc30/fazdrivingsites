COMMON QUESTIONS

How many lessons do I need to pass?

The DVSA says the average is 45 hours of professional tuition plus 22 hours of private practice. However, everyone learns at a different pace.

Do you offer weekend or evening lessons?

Yes! We offer flexible scheduling including evenings and weekends to fit around your work or college schedule.

Can you help me with the Theory Test?

Absolutely. All our students get free access to theory test training resources and guidance from Darren.
